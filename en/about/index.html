About the Company

SEC Elevators is a family-owned company and one of the Hussein Mohammed Jari Al-Zatari & Sons companies, specializing in maintenance, installation, and modernization of elevators, headquartered in Kuwait. Through commercial partnerships with the most prominent elevator companies in Kuwait, we provide integrated services from supply, design, and installation of villa and building elevators to service and maintenance contracts.

Our client base consists of real estate companies, commercial complexes, private and commercial buildings, private and investment villas, in addition to some government contracts.

Through our specialized technical team, you can receive advice regarding the right elevator for your needs. By working side by side with engineers and building contractors, we ensure that installation deadlines are met.

Our loyal and distinguished clients benefit from a range of advantages including special prices for our services and contracts.
